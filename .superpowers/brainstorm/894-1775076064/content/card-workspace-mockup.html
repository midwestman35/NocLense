How the Card Workspace works
  • 8 cards: Log Stream, AI Assistant, Evidence, Timeline (embedded in Log Stream), Similar Tickets, Correlation Graph, Datadog Live, Ticket Context (not shown — appears in Import/Submit views)
  • 3 preset layouts: Import (centered card + ticket lookup), Investigate (this grid), Submit (focused closure form + evidence summary)
  • Expand in-place: Click a card header to expand it — neighbors shrink with anime.js spring physics
  • Collapse to header: Double-click to collapse any card to just its header bar (one line)
  • Phase navigation: Top bar switches between Import → Investigate → Submit with layout morph animation
  • Ticket context: Always visible in header — priority badge, ticket ID, status. Not a separate card in Investigate.
